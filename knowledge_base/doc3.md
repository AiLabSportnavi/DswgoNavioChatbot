# Sportnavi Partner FAQ

---

## TOPIC: Partner Portal – Activation

**Q: How long does activation for the partner portal take?**
A: The review process typically takes a few days. Once complete, the profile is activated and the partner receives a confirmation email.

---

## TOPIC: Partner Portal – Access Link

**Q: Where can I find the link to the partner portal?**
A: The partner portal is available at: https://partner.sportnavi.de/

---

## TOPIC: Partner Portal – Features Overview

**Q: What can I do in the partner portal?**
A: The partner portal allows partners to:
- View and edit profile information (name, address, description) and submit change requests to Sportnavi
- View check-ins
- Download the location QR code
- Link additional locations

---

## TOPIC: Check-In – How It Works

**Q: How does the check-in process work at a partner location?**
A: Members check in via the Sportnavi app using a QR code or location confirmation.

Member requirements:
- A valid, active Sportnavi membership
- The latest version of the Sportnavi app, with the member logged in
- An active internet connection

Process: The member scans the partner's QR code using the app camera. A green confirmation screen appears, which the member shows to the partner. The partner should verify the member's name. A timer running at the bottom of the screen confirms the check-in is live and not a screenshot.

---

## TOPIC: Check-In – Frequency Limit

**Q: Can members check in multiple times per day?**
A: Yes. A new check-in can be registered every 2 hours.

---

## TOPIC: Check-In – Forgotten Check-In

**Q: A customer forgot to check in — what should the partner do?**
A: The partner is responsible for reviewing their own check-ins. In exceptional cases, the partner can contact Sportnavi, and the visit will be manually added to the system.

---

## TOPIC: Check-In – Technical Issues

**Q: The check-in via the app is not working — what should the partner do?**
A: Contact Sportnavi and describe the issue as precisely as possible. Helpful information includes:
- Does the issue affect all members or only one specific member?
- Since when has the problem been occurring?
The more detail provided, the faster the issue can be resolved.

---

## TOPIC: Bookings – No-Shows

**Q: A customer reserved a course or offer but did not show up — what happens?**
A: No-shows must be invoiced directly by the partner to the customer. Sportnavi is not involved in this process.

---

## TOPIC: Bookings – Member Cannot Book

**Q: A member cannot book an offer at the partner location — what should be done?**
A: Contact Sportnavi and describe the problem in detail so a solution can be found.

---

## TOPIC: Bookings – Cancellation Deadlines

**Q: Are there cancellation deadlines for bookings?**
A: Each partner defines their own cancellation policy for their offers independently.

---

## TOPIC: Profile Management – Changing Courses or Offerings

**Q: How does a partner change their courses or offerings?**
A: There are two options:
1. Email the partner management team at: partner@sportnavi.de
2. Use the partner portal via **Profile → Edit** to submit a change request (requests are processed internally by Sportnavi)

---

## TOPIC: Profile Management – Changing Logo

**Q: How does a partner change their logo?**
A: In the partner portal, navigate to **Profile → Edit**.

---

## TOPIC: Profile Management – Changing Profile or Description

**Q: How does a partner change their profile text or descriptions?**
A: In the partner portal, navigate to **Profile → Edit**.

---

## TOPIC: Offering – Pausing or Modifying

**Q: Can a partner pause or modify their offering?**
A: Yes. The partner contacts Sportnavi partner management, who will note the pause and arrange all further steps.

---

## TOPIC: Locations – Adding New Locations

**Q: How does a partner add a new location?**
A: Contact Sportnavi partner management or the sales representative from the original contract. Sportnavi will then reach out to clarify all further steps with the partner.

---

## TOPIC: QR Code – Lost or Stolen

**Q: What should a partner do if the QR code display is stolen or goes missing?**
A: Two options are available:
1. Download and print the QR code directly from the partner portal
2. Contact Sportnavi partner management (e.g. by email) to receive a new QR code sent at the start of the next month

---

## TOPIC: Payouts – Timing

**Q: When are payouts made to partners?**
A: 
- **Currently:** In the last week of the following month
- **Future plan:** By mid of the following month

---

## TOPIC: Payouts – Process

**Q: How does a payout work?**
A: The payout is transferred to the bank account specified in the cooperation agreement. The partner also receives a commission credit note as a PDF by email for saving, printing, and filing.

---

## TOPIC: Contract – Notice Period

**Q: Is there a notice period for partners?**
A: Yes. The standard notice period is 12 months. In some cases it is 3 months. The exact period applicable to each partner is stated in their individual cooperation agreement.

---

## TOPIC: Contract – How to Cancel

**Q: How can a partner cancel their contract?**
A: The cancellation request can be communicated to Sportnavi partner management or the responsible sales representative by email or phone.
